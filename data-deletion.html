Delete Your Elpis Account

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)
  1. Open the Elpis app on your device
  2. Tap the Profile icon (top right corner)
  3. Scroll down to find Account Settings
  4. Tap "Delete My Account"
  5. Read the confirmation message
  6. Tap "Yes, Delete My Account" to confirm

Your account will be deleted immediately.

Method 2: Request via Email
  1. Send an email with subject: "Delete My Account"
  2. Include your registered email address in the message
  3. We will process your request within 30 days
  4. You'll receive confirmation once deletion is complete
Data that will be permanently deleted:
  • Account credentials (email, password)
  • Profile information and profile picture
  • All journal entries
  • Breathing session records
  • Gratitude entries
  • Personal goals and progress
  • Achievements and rewards
  • Analytics history
  • Notification preferences

After Deletion

  • You will be logged out immediately
  • You cannot recover your data after deletion
  • You may create a new account using the same email if desired

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account or have questions, please contact us through the app's support feature.

Data Deletion Request

Elpis Wellness App

Your Right to Delete Your Data

At Elpis, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

What data will be deleted:
  • Your account information (email, name, profile picture)
  • All journal entries and reflections
  • Breathing session history
  • Gratitude entries
  • Goals and progress data
  • Achievement records
  • All analytics and usage data

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Delete from within the App
  1. Open the Elpis app
  2. Go to Profile (tap the user icon)
  3. Scroll down to Account Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your choice
Option 2: Email Request
  1. Send an email with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your Elpis account
  3. We will process your request within 30 days

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been permanently deleted.

Important Notes

  • Data deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • You will lose access to all your wellness history and achievements
  • You can create a new account after deletion if you wish to use Elpis again

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion or your privacy rights, please contact us through the app.
